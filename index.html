Our approach

A practical, hands on process built from founder and investor experience.

Step 1 – Discovery

Initial review of company, market, current materials, and capital needs.

Step 2 – Structure

Align on round structure, use of funds, milestones, and investor expectations.

Step 3 – Materials

Create or refine the deck, one page, and financial overview plus a clear data room outline.

Step 4 – Outreach

Support targeted outreach, responses to questions, and ongoing communication through closing.
